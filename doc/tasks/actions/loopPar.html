loopPar

Task Action: LoopPar

The loopPar action is similar than loop action with the ability to leverage multi-core infrastructure. Therefore the subTask of the loopPar action will be executed in CONCURRENCE.

WARNING: to avoid variable conclict during parralel execution, we encourage users to use defineVar methods to ensure varaible scopes.
